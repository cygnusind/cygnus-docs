Cancellation and Refund Policy

1. Flight Cancellations

1.1. Cancellation charges will vary based on:

  • Airline policies
  • Fare type
  • Timing of cancellation

1.2. Service fee charged by Cygnus Voyages is non-refundable.

2. Hotel Cancellations

2.1. Hotel cancellation policies vary by property and rate type.
2.2. No-show charges may apply as per hotel policy.

3. Refund Processing

3.1. Refund timeline:

  • Airlines: 7-14 business days after airline approval
  • Hotels: 5-10 business days after hotel approval
  • Other services: As per service provider's policy

4. Cancellation Fees

4.1. Corporate clients will be charged as per their service agreement.
4.2. Standard cancellation fees:

  • More than 72 hours: 10% of booking value
  • 48-72 hours: 25% of booking value
  • 24-48 hours: 50% of booking value
  • Less than 24 hours: 100% of booking value

5. Force Majeure

5.1. Special considerations for cancellations due to:

  • Natural disasters
  • Political unrest
  • Pandemic-related restrictions
  • Other unforeseen circumstances

6. Refund Methods

6.1. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method.
6.2. Corporate clients may opt for credit to their account.
